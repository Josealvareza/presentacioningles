Cola de mono

It is typical of Chilean cuisine. It is widely consumed at Christmas and New Year, often accompanied by Easter bread. Ingredients: Vodka, yolks, nescafe, cinnamon and milk.

Charquicán

El charquicán, ideal for cold climates. Its name comes from Charqui, the dried meat covered with salt and with which the charquicán is prepared. ingredients: Meat, pumpkin, onion, corn and chili pepper.


Approximate price: 15.00 USD

Cazuela

It is a very delicious dish and consumed more frequently in the beautiful days of winter or for people who are fascinated by the broth to cover the cold. Ingredients: Brisket, brown paprika, celery, corn, red potatoes and chicken.


Approximate price: 10.00 USD

Caldillo

El caldillo de congrio es un plato de la gastronomía chilena elaborado a base de congrio dorado (Genypterus blacodes) o congrio colorado (Genypterus chilensis), un pescado que abunda en la costa del Pacífico sudamericano. Lo que lo hace particular internacionalmente es que fue inmortalizada en forma de poesía por el poeta chileno Pablo Neruda, lo que fomentó en Chile su preparación, principalmente en restaurantes dedicados al turismo. Ingredientes: Congrio, langosta y verduras.


Approximate price: 25.00 USD
