Types of weather in Chile

  • Cold
  • Warm
  • Autumn
  • tropical climate

Polar or cold

weather Chile during the winter is tremendously frozen, not only because of its low temperatures, rain and snow, but because the wind is freezing and there are always polar waves. The minimum temperature (early in the morning) will be around 2 ° C and possibly on many occasions, it will fall below 0 ° C. The maximum temperature with luck will be about 17 ° C.

What kind of clothes to wear: Jeans, cotelé, thick cloth pants will be necessary to survive this era. Thick sheepskin jackets with fur or chiporro, waterproof padded parkas and coats of thick material. It is essential to wear a scarf. Wear wool or leather gloves.

Warm

When summer comes, the heat comes and it is best that you know what clothes you use to keep yourself cool.

Type of clothes for warm weather: sneakers, short-sleeved blouses, dresses and skirts, shorts, warm clothes, sunglasses.

Autumn

In autumn the weather conditions can vary a lot, so it is best to be attentive and prepare for any situation. You can expect cold breezes, sunny days and very cold nights.

tropical climate

If you are traveling to a tropical paradise, you should think about time when deciding what to wear. Basically, it will be very hot, which means you need clothes made of natural fibers that are very breathable and absorbent. Leave your polyester and wool blends at home; In tropical climates, you should use light and comfortable fabrics.

Try to wear clothes made of silk, cotton and linen. They can be shirts, shorts, shorts, short shirts.
